FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I search for a product?

Go to the relevant category and use the search bar to search by using appropriate keywords. Please ensure that you have selected the appropriate location so that you see nearby options first.

How can I find ads that I have viewed before but not chosen yet?

Have you seen something interesting? If you’re busy, you can get back to it, at your convenience.  You can add a product to your wishlist & view it later.

Where can I see the ads that I have posted?

Firstly, ensure you are logged in the app with the user details you used while posting the Ads. Go to your profile and you will be able to find the listings you have posted.

What can I do if I find a post to be fake?

We want to ensure that you have an authentic and safe experience on our platform. If you find and posts that are fake or inauthentic, you will be able to report the problem and state the reason right under the post.
